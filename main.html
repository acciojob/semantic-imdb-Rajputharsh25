Summary

3 Idiots is a story about three engineering students who form a deep friendship while studying in college. The movie highlights problems in the education system and encourages students to follow their passion. Rancho, the main character, inspires his friends to think creatively and live life fearlessly.

Actors

The main actors in this movie are Aamir Khan as Rancho, R. Madhavan as Farhan, Sharman Joshi as Raju, Kareena Kapoor as Pia, and Boman Irani as Virus. Their performances made the movie emotional, inspirational, and entertaining.

Favorite Quote

"All is well." This quote teaches us to stay calm and confident even during difficult situations in life.
